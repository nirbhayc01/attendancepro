Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Attendance Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and stored when you use our mobile application. We built this app with a privacy-first architecture.

1. Data We Collect & Storage

Attendance Pro is a "Local-First" application. This means the vast majority of your data never leaves your personal device.

  • Local Data: Your attendance history, subjects, timetable, tasks, and calendar events are stored exclusively in your device's local browser storage. We do not have access to, nor do we collect, this personal data.
  • Timetable Sharing (Cloud): If you choose to generate a "Share Code" to share your schedule with classmates, your timetable structure (times and subject names) is temporarily uploaded to our secure database. This data contains no personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Push Notifications: If you enable daily reminders, an anonymous, system-generated notification token is securely stored so our servers can send a reminder ping to your device at 5 PM.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase as our backend database purely for enabling the Timetable Sharing and Push Notification features. Supabase is fully compliant with modern global data protection and security standards.

3. Your Rights & Data Control

Because your primary attendance history is stored locally on your hardware, you have complete and absolute control over it. You can delete all your data instantly at any time by using the "Factory Reset App" option located in the app's Settings menu. This will wipe the local storage entirely.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
